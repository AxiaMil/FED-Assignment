Tobacco laws in Singapore

Tobacco (Control of Advertisements and Sale) (Labelling - Exemption) Order 2019

Order 2019 permits the sale of tobacco products that are compliant with the Tobacco (Control of Advertisements and Sale) (Appearance, Packaging and Labelling) Regulations 2019 to be sold in the three months (April 1, 2020 to June 30, 2020) leading up to those regulations entering into force (July 1, 2020).
This mainly includes:

  • “cigar tube” means a retail package of a cigar that complies with regulation 19;
  • “cigarette carton” means a retail package of cigarettes which is a box or carton containing 2 or more cigarette packs;
  • “cigarette pack” means a retail package of cigarettes which contains not less than the prescribed minimum number of cigarettes in section 12(3) of the Act;
  • “health information message” means the health information message mentioned in regulation 25(2);
  • “decorative feature” includes any ridge, bulge or bevel;
  • “health warning” means a health warning mentioned in regulation 21(2) or (3), 22(1) or 23(1);
  • “import and wholesale licensee” means the holder of an import and wholesale licence mentioned in section 18(1) or (2) of the Act;
  • “larger package” means 2 or more retail packages of any tobacco product fastened or wrapped together without an outer container;
  • “mandatory sign” means a sign bearing — (a) a health warning; (b) the health information message; or 3 S 480/2019 (c) the sales restriction information;

Tobacco (Control of Advertisements and Sale) (Specified Premises - Exemption) Order 2017

This Order is the Tobacco (Control of Advertisements and Sale) (Specified Premises — Exemption) Order 2017 and comes into operation on 1 January 2018.
Definitions
In this Order — “import and wholesale licensee” means the holder of an import and wholesale licence mentioned in section 18(1) or (2) of the Act; “retail licensee” means the holder of a retail licence mentioned in section 18(3) of the Act; “specified premises”, in relation to a licence, means the premises specified in the licence.
Exemption
(1) Section 18(4)(a) of the Act does not apply to an import and wholesale licensee that does any of the following at premises other than specified premises:

  • (a) distributes by wholesale any tobacco product;
  • (b) sells or offers for sale by wholesale any tobacco product;
  • (c) permits to be sold or offered for sale by wholesale any
    • tobacco product;
  • (d) has in the licensee’s possession any tobacco product for
    • sale by wholesale, if those premises are authorised premises.
(2) For the purposes of sub‑paragraph (1), the authorised premises are —
  • (a) in the case of a supply to another import and wholesale licensee, the premises specified in that other licensee’s licence; or
  • (b) in the case of a supply to a retail licensee, the premises specified in the retail licensee’s licence.
(3) Regulation 11
  • (1) of the Tobacco (Control of Advertisements and Sale) (Licensing) Regulations 2017 (G.N. No. S 763/2017) does not apply to an import and wholesale licensee that stores or places, or causes to be stored or placed, at the authorised premises mentioned in sub‑paragraph
  • (2), any tobacco product that the licensee intends to distribute or sell by wholesale at those premises.

Tobacco (Control of Advertisements and Sale) (Exemption for Duty-free Retailers and Specialist Tobacconists) Order 2017

In this Order — “Changi Airport” means the airport declared under the Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore (Changi Airport) Notification 2009 (G.N. No. S 293/2009); Informal Consolidation – version in force from 1/8/2017 “cigar shop” means a retail outlet where, at any time —

  • (a) more than 50% of the products displayed for sale in the outlet are tobacco products; and
  • (b) at least 90% of the tobacco products in paragraph (a) are cigars; “duty-free retailer” means a person that holds —
    • (a) a valid retail licence under the Act to distribute, sell or offer for sale any tobacco product by retail at a retail outlet specified in the retail licence; and
    • (b) a valid licence granted under section 82 of the Customs Act (Cap. 70) to sell goods free of duty to tourists and returning or departing residents of Singapore at a retail outlet designated in that licence, being the same retail outlet mentioned in paragraph
      • (a); “permitted information”, in relation to a tobacco product, means any of the following:
      • (a) the price of the tobacco product;
      • (b) the name of the manufacturer or brand name of the tobacco product;
  • (c) the type or variant of the tobacco product;
  • (d) the net weight, or number of units in each package, of the tobacco product;
  • (e) the barcode or stock number of the tobacco product; “plainly display”, for a tobacco product, means to display the tobacco product in such a way that the display
    • (a) is not accompanied by any images, sound, light or text, other than the permitted information in plain text on a price tag, if any; or
    • (b) is not otherwise intended to induce, urge, promote or encourage the use of the tobacco product;
    Informal Consolidation – version in force from 1/8/2017 “specialist tobacconist” means a person that holds a valid retail licence under the Act to distribute, sell or offer for sale any tobacco product by retail at a cigar shop specified in that licence.
